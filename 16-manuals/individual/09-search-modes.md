## Outline

- Three ways to find jobs and when to use each:
  - **All Jobs** — when you want to browse the full market (posted + aggregated)
  - **Auto Match** — when you trust your profile and want AI to surface fits
  - **Manual Search** — when you have specific filters in mind (salary, location, remote)
- Aggregated jobs vs platform-posted jobs (and why match scores differ)
- Reading match scores and ghost-risk scores
