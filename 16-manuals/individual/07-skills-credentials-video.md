# Chapter 7 — Adding skills, credentials, and a video intro

> _Not yet drafted. See [[08-questions/individual-training-manual-plan]] for the schedule._

## Outline

- Skills with proficiency levels (vs. just listing keywords)
- Skill Verifications: take a test, earn a verified badge visible to recruiters
- Credentials: certifications, education, licences — with expiry reminders
- Video Profile: 60–90 second intro, what to include, what to avoid
- Portfolio Builder: when it matters (creative, technical, consulting roles)
