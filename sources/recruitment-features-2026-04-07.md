# Source: Recruitment Features Guide

**Raw file:** `raw/articles/recruitment-features.md`
**Ingested:** 2026-04-07

## Summary

A comprehensive guide detailing 95+ features available to Recruiter users on Sebenza Hub, structured as "what it is / how to use it / why it matters". Covers Recruitment, Evaluation, AI Tools, Talent & Engagement, Scheduling & Communications, Analytics, Revenue Engine, Business tools, and Governance.

## Key Claims

### Recruitment Core (9 features)
- **Profile** — Recruiter identity with specialisations and placement history
- **Jobs** — Full job management: create, publish, track from intake to fill
- **Pipeline** — Visual Kanban board with drag-and-drop stage management
- **Screening Roles** — Pre-configured auto-screening criteria per role type
- **Candidates** — Complete candidate database with full search and segmentation
- **Talent CRM** — Relationship management with drip campaigns and engagement tracking
- **AI Sourcing** — AI-powered candidate discovery with ranked shortlists and match scores
- **Rediscovery** — Resurfaces silver-medal candidates from past roles for new matches
- **LinkedIn Leads** — Import and track LinkedIn sourcing activity

### Evaluation (20+ features)
- **Compare** — Side-by-side candidate comparison on structured criteria
- **Scorecards** — Standardised interview evaluation forms with aggregated scoring
- **Offers** — Full offer lifecycle: create, approve, send, track
- **Offer Letters** — Branded, dynamic-field letter generation
- **Offer Analytics** — Acceptance rates, decline reasons, negotiation frequency
- **Negotiations** — Structured workspace for salary/benefits negotiation
- **Offer Templates, Offer Compare, Offer Timeline, Offer Compliance, Benchmarking, Offer Approvals**
- **Tests, Test Library, Test Templates** — 300+ pre-built assessments
- **Interview Kits** — Structured packages with questions and evaluation criteria
- **Video Interviews** — Live and asynchronous (one-way recorded)
- **Background Checks** — Criminal, employment, qualification, credit verification
- **Blind Hiring** — Anonymised candidate data during screening

### AI Tools (8 features)
- **AI JD Writer** — Generate inclusive job descriptions from minimal input
- **AI Questions** — Role-specific interview question generation
- **AI Search** — Natural language candidate search
- **Screening Bot** — Automated initial screening chatbot (24/7)
- **Predictive Hiring** — ML models for offer acceptance, tenure, performance prediction
- **Culture Fit Intel** — Values and working style alignment assessment
- **Video Analysis** — AI analysis of video interview communication and confidence
- **AI Candidate Summary** — Distils CVs into concise overviews (10 seconds vs 7 minutes)

### Talent & Engagement (6 features)
- **Referrals** — Employee/network referral management
- **Candidate NPS** — Net Promoter Score surveys at key recruitment touchpoints
- **Onboarding** — Structured workflows bridging offer to first day
- **Talent Pools** — Curated candidate segments for future roles
- **Re-Engagement** — Automated campaigns for dormant candidates
- **Reference Checks** — Digital reference collection and verification

### Scheduling & Communications (6 features)
- **Unified Inbox** — Aggregates email, SMS, WhatsApp, in-platform messages
- **Smart Messaging** — AI-assisted drafting with tone analysis and sentiment detection
- **Chatbot** — Career site chatbot for candidate FAQs and applications
- **Self-Scheduling** — Candidate-controlled interview booking
- **Email Templates, Sequences** — Automated multi-step outreach

### Analytics (8 features)
- **Overview, Predictive, Team DNA, Panel Analysis, Engagement Score, Diversity Analytics, Requirement Check, Offer Predictor**

### Revenue Engine (6 features)
- **Client Pipeline** — CRM for tracking client relationships and revenue
- **Submissions** — Track candidate submissions to clients
- **Placements** — End-to-end placement tracking with invoicing
- **KPI Dashboard** — Real-time performance metrics
- **Call Notes** — Structured phone call logging
- **Rapid Workflow** — Fast-track process for urgent roles

### Business & Governance (9 features)
- **Clients, Career Site, Marketing, Job Boards, Brand Page, Location Intel**
- **WhatsApp Apply, WhatsApp Command Center, WA Campaigns**
- **Approvals, Audit Trail, Automation**
