# Source: Individual Features Guide

**Raw file:** `raw/articles/individual-features.md`
**Ingested:** 2026-04-07

## Summary

A comprehensive guide detailing every feature available to Individual users (job seekers) on Sebenza Hub, structured as "what it is / how to use it / why it matters" for each feature. Covers 56+ features across Profile, Job Search, Applications, AI Tools, Learning Hub, Career Development, and Community.

## Key Claims

> **Note:** Feature counts below were corrected after the PDF version of this guide was ingested. See [[sources/individual-feature-guide-pdf-2026-04-07]] for the reconciliation.

### Profile & Identity (5 features)
- **My CVs** — Multi-version CV management, tailored to different industries
- **Credentials** — Verified digital wallet for qualifications with trust badges and expiry reminders
- **Video Profile** — 60–90 second video introduction visible to recruiters
- **Skill Verifications** — Standardised tests that add verified badges to skills
- **CV Templates** — Library of professionally designed templates

### Job Search (6 features)
- **All Jobs** — Centralised job board with multi-filter search
- **Auto Match** — AI-powered continuous job matching with notifications
- **Manual Search** — Advanced filters (location, salary, industry, remote/hybrid/onsite)
- **Saved Searches** — Persistent search criteria with new-match alerts
- **Favourites** — Personal job shortlist for comparison
- **Company Research** — Company profiles with culture, size, reviews, hiring activity

### Applications (10 features)
- **My Applications** — Dashboard of all applications with status tracking
- **App Tracker** — Visual timeline of each application's progress
- **App Analytics** — Personal success metrics (response rates, conversion)
- **Notes & Tags** — Personal CRM for job applications
- **Reminders** — Customisable alerts for follow-ups and deadlines
- **Application Portal** — Unified portal for employer requests
- **Candidate Hub** — Aggregated dashboard of priorities and actions
- **Interview Scheduling** — Self-service slot selection with calendar integration
- **Experience Surveys** — Post-process feedback surveys (anonymous)
- **Messages** — In-platform threaded messaging with recruiters/employers

### AI Tools (4 features)
- **AI Coaching** — On-demand personalised career guidance
- **CV Review** — Detailed CV analysis with actionable feedback
- **Cover Letter** — AI-generated role-specific cover letters
- **Salary Negotiator** — Data-driven negotiation prep with market data

### Learning Hub (14 features)
- **Learning Dashboard** — Progress, achievements, recommendations
- **Learning Paths** — Curated course sequences for specific skills/roles
- **Skill Tree** — Visual skill map with levels and growth opportunities
- **Micro Learning** — 5–15 minute focused modules
- **Interview Simulator** — AI mock interviews with feedback
- **Job-Linked Learning** — Skills gap courses tied to specific job listings
- **Challenges** — Project-based assessments that become portfolio pieces
- **Portfolio Builder** — Showcase work, projects, and achievements
- **Peer Teaching** — Teach skills you've mastered, learn from others
- **SETA Learnerships** — SA-specific accredited learnership programmes
- **Certifications** — Professional certification catalogue with exam prep
- **Study Planner** — Personal study scheduling with reminders
- **Learning Analytics** — Time spent, modules completed, skill growth
- **Mentorship** — Structured programme with mentor matching and session scheduling

### Career Development (6 features)
- **Skill Gap** — Gap analysis between current skills and target role requirements
- **Career Path** — Visual career trajectories with skills and timeline
- **Salary Insights** — Market salary data by title, industry, experience, location
- **Career Pulse** — Periodic satisfaction check-ins with sentiment tracking
- **Tests** — Skills and knowledge assessments with verified badges
- **Analytics** — Personal career dashboard with market competitiveness

### Community (6 features)
- **Community Forum** — Discussion forum for advice, experiences, support
- **Trending** — Curated feed of trending topics and content
- **Leaderboard** — Gamified ranking by contributions and engagement
- **Interview Vault** — Shared repository of real interview questions by company
- **Mentor Connect** — Matchmaking with experienced professionals
- **Badges** — Digital achievement badges visible to recruiters

### Account (2 features)
- **Billing** — Subscription and payment management
- **Settings** — Privacy, notifications, integrations, profile visibility

- SETA learnership integration is unique to the SA market and differentiates from global competitors.
- The credential wallet with verification badges and trust scoring is a strong signal of the platform's focus on data quality.
