# Individual Journey Gap Analysis

The journey document covers the **core hiring flow well**, but it's missing a significant number of features. Here's the gap analysis:

## Covered in the Journey

- Signup & Authentication
- Onboarding
- Profile, CVs, Credentials, Video Profile
- Job Search (All Jobs, Auto Match, Manual Search, Saved Searches, Favourites, Company Research)
- Applications & Tracking (Applications, Tracker, Analytics, Notes, Reminders, Inbox)
- Interview Scheduling & Interview Simulator
- AI Cover Letter, Salary Negotiator
- Learning Hub (briefly in Step 8)
- Community (briefly in Step 8)
- Career Pulse & Skill Verifications (briefly in Step 8)

## Missing from the Journey (30+ features)

### My Profile Section

- **CV Templates** — browse/favourite CV templates
- **Skill Verifications** — mentioned briefly but not in the profile-building step

### Applications Section

- **Application Portal** — dedicated portal view
- **Candidate Hub** — enhanced candidate portal
- **Experience Surveys** — post-interview experience feedback

### AI Tools Section (entirely missing as a section)

- **AI Coaching** — career coaching AI
- **CV Review** — AI-powered CV analysis

### Learning Hub (14 sub-features, only mentioned generically)

- **Learning Paths**, **Skill Tree**, **Micro Learning**
- **Job-Linked Learning**, **Learning Challenges**
- **Portfolio Builder**, **Peer Teaching**
- **SETA Learnerships** (18 tabs — finder, directory, tracker, eligibility, NQF explorer, stipend calculator, etc.)
- **Certifications**, **Study Planner**, **Learning Analytics**, **Mentorship**

### Career Development Section (entirely missing)

- **Skill Gap** analysis
- **Career Path** planning
- **Salary Insights** (market data, not just negotiation)
- **Career Tests**
- **Career Analytics** (5 tabs: overview, performance, insights, market, predictive)

### Community Section (6 features, only mentioned generically)

- **Community Forum** (11 tabs including feed, trending, bookmarks, success stories, challenges, pulse)
- **Leaderboard**, **Interview Vault**, **Mentor Connect**, **Badges**

### Knowledge Base Section (entirely missing)

- **My Wikis** — personal knowledge base with wiki dashboard, viewer, and pages

### Account Section (missing details)

- **Billing** (7 tabs: overview, value/ROI, credits, budget, invoices, plans, settings)
- **Settings** details (8 tabs: preferences, career DNA, automation, notifications, appearance, privacy, security, account)

### Profile Page Depth (missing tabs)

- Work History, Education, Integrations, Documents, Endorsements, Conversations, Public Profile

## Verdict

The journey document covers roughly **40% of the actual features**. It nails the linear "signup to hired" path but misses the breadth of the platform — particularly Career Development, Learning Hub depth, Community, Knowledge Base, AI Tools, and Account/Billing.
