# Recruiter Journey Gap Analysis

**Source:** Internal analysis comparing [[03-workflows/recruiter-journey]] against [[01-entities/recruiter-user]] and [[09-sources/recruitment-features-2026-04-07]]
**Analysed:** 2026-04-08

## Summary

The Recruiter journey document covered the linear recruitment path well (Steps 1–10: signup through placement and retention) but missed ~35 features across communication, analytics, compliance, and brand/growth categories. Coverage was approximately 60% of the full Recruiter feature set.

## Coverage Gaps by Category

| Category | In Journey | Missing | Gap Severity |
|----------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Communication & Automation | Sequences, re-engagement | Unified Inbox, Smart Messaging, Email Templates, Workflow Automation, WhatsApp Command Center, WA Campaigns, Chatbot (career site) | High |
| Analytics & Intelligence | KPI dashboard, basic analytics | Team DNA, Panel Analysis, Engagement Scores, Diversity Analytics, Requirement Check, AI Candidate Summary, AI Intelligence | High |
| Compliance & Governance | B-BBEE in profile only | Audit Trail, Approval Workflows (general), POPIA tools, EE Reports, Diversity reporting | High |
| Brand & Growth | None | Career Site, Brand Page, Job Board Integrations, Recruitment Marketing (detail), Referrals, Candidate NPS, Location Intel, Reference Checks | Critical |
| Account & Ops | None | Billing, Settings, Onboarding (post-hire), Rapid Workflow | Medium |

## Resolution

Restructured from 10 linear steps to **10 recruitment steps + 4 parallel tracks** with scenario-driven narratives:
- **Track A: Communication & Automation** — candidates going cold, managing 30 active candidates, eliminating repetitive tasks
- **Track B: Analytics & Intelligence** — slow fills, inconsistent panels, proving agency value, AI shortcuts
- **Track C: Compliance & Governance** — EE audit, POPIA data request, fee dispute
- **Track D: Brand & Growth** — inbound candidate attraction, new market expansion, referral-driven growth
